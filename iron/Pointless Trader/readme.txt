Pointless Trader

In this text game, your goal is to become as rich as possible. Sell and buy gold at the right prices
once a place has no gold left or is too expensive, you can leave in search for a better location.

This game is written in LUA, and was made for the STDIO game jam
hosted by the Museum of Art and Digital Entertainment

Measures 230 lines of code, according to CLOC
can be played at http://www.tutorialspoint.com/execute_lua_online.php
or in any lua interpreter supporting user input.
